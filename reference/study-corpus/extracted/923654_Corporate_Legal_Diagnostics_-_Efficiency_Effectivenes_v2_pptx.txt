Corporate Legal & Compliance function data gathering guidelines Data should  include  spend related to:  Salaries and benefits of personnel working in the function as defined by the taxonomy Travel and entertainment for personnel in the function  External law firm fees (fees for Law Firm services themselves (i.e., the time and assets of the law firm) Databases and productivity tools used by the function (e.g., knowledge databases, e-billing tool or other software or data providers) Supplies and subscriptions used by the function Data should  exclude  spend related to:   Settlement fees or fines paid by the organization directly Any pass- through costs such as settlements paid by the law firm on the client’s behalf Fines/penalties paid for to the government Large enterprise systems (e.g., ERP systems, etc.)
