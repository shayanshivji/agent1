profitability, etc.)

proposition for each segment

impact have been identified
There’s monitoring of budget execution across financial  (e.g., revenues, costs, etc.)

Business 
processes
The S&OP process is mainly based on maximization of freight’s marginal income rather 
complex modeling of marginal income across the fleet (i.e., taking into account empty 
runs and duration of the freight)
The S&OP process is based on modeling cargo base scenarios with maximization of 
marginal income for the fleet (with modeling of various opportunities for reducing empty 
runs)
All customer-related processes are focused on the commercial component (tariffs, 
fines, etc.)
Customer-related processes are focused on identifying win-win opportunities (e.g., 
reducing duration of loading and unloading operations)
The process of searching and attracting clients is mainly based on an "incoming" flow of 
requests with the same offering for all customers (e.g., a standard contract, etc.)
The process of searching and attracting clients consists of market analysis and the 
decisions around customer segments (e.g., anchor clients, optimizers, HiPo), followed 
by setting goals and creation of differentiated offering
The performance management process is mainly focused on the analysis of financial 
and high-level operational indicators (e.g., freight volume)
Performance management process is focused on root cause analysis and corrective 
measures development
Org 
structure
The functions of planning, sales, transportation and performance management are 
concentrated in the commercial department
The functions of planning, sales, transportation and performance management are 
separate and independent
The organization is built around the fleet – maturity of the marketing function is at a 
basic level
The organization works in the customer-centric logic  – maturity of the marketing 
function is at a high level

3. Example: key elements of performance management process
KPI tree
KPI 
targets
Analysis 
of the 
results
Elements
EBITDA tree is developed
The target values of KPIs are set on the basis of ambitious goal setting and full potential assessment
Monitoring and factor analysis of financial indicators and operational drivers is carried out
Based on EBITDA tree operational drivers with maximum impact are identified (e.g., empty run, wagon 
turnover, etc.)
Responsibility for the elements of EBITDA tree and key operational drivers is assigned to the relevant 
structural units and employees
Achieving the KPI target values by departments ensures the achievement of the Company's target
